About RNH Welding

Established in 2018, RNH Welding has rapidly emerged as a leading player in the welding and fabrication industry. With over 20 years of collective experience in the structural steel industry, RNH Welding has successfully completed numerous projects of various scales and complexities.

Our team of highly skilled professionals possesses a wealth of knowledge and expertise, enabling us to deliver exceptional welding and fabrication services across a wide range of sectors. From hotels and schools to custom houses, hand railings, and guard railings of all types and assortments, we have a diverse portfolio that showcases our versatility and craftsmanship.

At RNH Welding, we take immense pride in our commitment to excellence. All our welders are WABO (Washington Association of Building Officials) certified, and we place great emphasis on staying updated with the latest welding technology and procedures. By staying at the forefront of advancements in the industry, we ensure that our clients benefit from the highest quality and most efficient welding techniques available.

Conveniently located in Chehalis, Washington, RNH Welding enjoys easy access to major cities such as Seattle and Portland, as well as the surrounding areas. This strategic location allows us to efficiently serve clients throughout the region, contributing to our reputation as a trusted and reliable partner in the welding and fabrication industry.

One of the cornerstones of our success is our collaborative approach. We have cultivated strong partnerships with other fabrication shops in our industry, enabling us to undertake larger and more complex projects seamlessly. Our reputation as the go-to fabrication shop that gets the job done right has been built on consistently delivering superior quality workmanship and exceeding client expectations.
